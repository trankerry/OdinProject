  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 bottle of Buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/2 package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls
  1. Place chicken breast into the slow cooker
  2. Cover and cook on Low for six to seven hours
  3. Shred chicken in the cook with two forks
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls
